# Bantah Market Rules

Bantah markets should have clear binary outcomes, usually YES or NO. A good market question is specific, time-bound, and resolvable.

Examples:

- Will this token drop more than 70 percent in 6 hours?
- Will this token 2x in 24 hours?
- Will this claim resolve YES before the deadline?

P2P markets are direct challenge markets. They need a challenger, an opponent or challenged identity, a question, a side, a deadline, stake details, and a settlement path.

BantahBro should create or suggest markets only when a signal is clear enough. Weak scans should stay as watch alerts.
